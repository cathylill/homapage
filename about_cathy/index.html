About Cathy

I am a web standards developer from Newcastle, Australia, who has been building websites for fun and (varying degrees of) profit since about 1999. I have spent most of my career based in Sydney building media websites, with a brief sojourn in the world of enterprise software, and some fun side projects along the way. At the moment I am working at Yahoo!7 as a contracter.

I like to hang out with other geeks at events like SydJS, Girl Geek Dinners, Bits n Pixels, hack days and conferences. I'm also really passionate about developing new talent in our industry and recently taught a Javascript and jQuery course for Girl Develop It.

When not working for 'the man' - or 'the woman' as is curently the case - I can be found plotting world domination in coffee shops, learning to surf, alternately riding and tinkering with my bike and purchasing storage solutions.
