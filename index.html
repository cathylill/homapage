Cathy Lill

Creator of tasty web goodness.

I am a Front End / Javascript developer who has been building websites for fun and (mostly) profit since Netscape was a thing. I am currently a Senior Software Engineer at Data61 / CSIRO working on a WebRTC based communications app called Coviu. Some of my other projects include the Canvas WYSIWYG email editor at Campaign Monitor, the responsive redesign for Yahoo!7 network, creating interactive components for the news.com.au network and rebuilding the front end for Collins Dictionary.

I like to hang out with other geeks at events like SydJS, Girl Geek Dinners, Bits n Pixels, hack days and conferences. I work from home in my basement in sunny Newcastle.
